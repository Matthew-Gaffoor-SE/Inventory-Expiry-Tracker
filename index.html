Inventory Expiry Tracker

Privacy Policy

Inventory Expiry Tracker helps users keep track of food and drink items and monitor their expiry dates. The app stores the information you add, such as product names, expiry dates, and shared lists, so your inventory can be accessed and managed across devices.

Data We Collect

Inventory Expiry Tracker stores information you choose to add, including food and drink items, expiry dates, and barcode data used to identify products. This information is used only to provide the core functionality of the app.

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a cloud backend service that allows your lists and inventory items to be saved and synchronized across devices. Supabase may process limited technical information required to operate the service.

Notifications

Inventory Expiry Tracker may send notifications to remind you when items in your inventory are approaching their expiry date. These reminders are based on the expiry dates you enter into the app.

Shared Lists

The app allows you to share your inventory lists with friends, family, or household members. Items added to a shared list will be visible to all users who have access to that list.

Barcode Scanning

When using the barcode scanning feature, the app may retrieve publicly available product information to help identify items. No personal data is collected during the scanning process.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements or changes to the app. Any updates will be posted on this page.
